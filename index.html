1. Introduction

Welcome to Tasbeeh Tracker – a simple, offline digital tasbeeh / zikr counter app developed for personal use by Muslims to keep track of dhikr.

We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains that we collect no personal data, no usage data, no location, no device information, and no analytics from you.

2. Information We Collect

We collect ZERO personal information.

  • No account creation is required.
  • No email, name, phone number or any identifier is asked.
  • No internet permission is used (app is 100% offline).
  • No camera, microphone, location, storage (except app's own local preferences), or any other dangerous permission is requested.
  • The only data stored is your current count and target value – saved locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences (not sent anywhere).

3. How We Use Information

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to use, share or sell.

The locally saved count and target are only used to remember your last session when you reopen the app.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any information with any third parties because we do not collect any.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools (Firebase, Google Analytics, etc.), ads, crash reporting, or any external services.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.

7. Changes to This Policy
